Giving AI Images, Not Just Words

Multimodal AI research write-up

This recent public project explores a practical shift in AI workflows: when a problem is visual, giving the model an image can be more precise than translating everything into text first.

The write-up compares text-only prompting with image-assisted reasoning across chart analysis, visual token compression, and industrial equipment question answering.

Key takeaway: text is strongest for intent and constraints, while images are strongest for context, evidence, spatial relationships, and attention.
